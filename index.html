How to Use Clenora Toilet Cleaner

Directions for Use

1

Press & Apply

Press the cap and apply under the rim of the toilet bowl

2

Wait 5 Minutes

Let the cleaner work on tough stains and germs

3

Brush & Flush

Brush thoroughly and flush for a clean, fresh toilet

Safety Precautions

  • Contains strong cleaning agents - avoid direct contact with skin and eyes
  • Keep out of reach of children and pets
  • Do not mix with other cleaning products
  • Use in well-ventilated areas
  • In case of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with water
  • Wear gloves while using for best protection
⚠ Do not use on marble or natural stone surfaces
